## Health system operations solutions

Align staff, beds, and resources across the care continuum to strengthen health system operations. Anticipate demand, guide patient journeys, and deliver connected, patient-centered care at every stage.

### Clinical location awareness

#### Track patients, clinicians, and equipment in near real time

Automate assignments and equipment tracking to help boost productivity, streamline workflows, and strengthen infection control.

##### EHR-agnostic insights across your enterprise

Oracle Health Clinical Location Awareness is EHR-agnostic and integrates seamlessly with your existing solutions to deliver near real-time location services. Gain a unified view of patients, clinicians, and equipment to expand operational awareness.

##### Geographic insight into assets and care environments

Oracle Health Clinical Location Awareness delivers venue-specific views and geographic mapping of patients, staff, and equipment for near real-time visibility, helping leaders spot trends, optimize investments, and make smart resource decisions.

### Clinical operations whiteboard

#### Transform care with a digital whiteboard

Integrate with our EHR to gain near real-time visibility into patient flow, staffing, and critical activities. Streamline documentation, support collaboration, and equip leaders with insights to optimize bed use, length of stay, discharge planning, and resource management.

##### EHR integration for quick insights

Oracle Health Clinical Operations Whiteboard integrates with our EHR to automatically populate events and orders, enabling care teams with a consolidated view of relevant patient details to help support timely, informed decisions.

##### Streamline bed management

Oracle Health Clinical Operations Whiteboard centralizes bed assignments into a structured, unitwide view, providing teams with the visibility and consistency to manage patient flow, coordinate transitions, and keep discharges on track.

##### Early discharge barrier detection

Oracle Health Clinical Operations Whiteboard offers leaders visibility into clinical details and patient-to-staff assignments, helping them identify discharge barriers early in the stay and coordinate smooth, timely transitions.

### Command center dashboard

#### Streamline patient flow, forecast needs, connect teams

Gain enterprisewide visibility with near real-time insights, apply predictive analytics, and centralize operations to help empower leaders to make timely decisions, optimize resources, and coordinate patient-centered care.

##### Enterprise bed capacity insight

Oracle Health Command Center Dashboard leverages near real-time data and predictive analytics to provide a clear view of enterprise bed capacity by level of care, helping leaders align staff, beds, and equipment to meet demand efficiently.

##### Align staffing with demand

Oracle Health Command Center Dashboard delivers centralized insights into staffing demand and scheduled supply in near real time, enabling leaders to balance resources, adapt quickly, and respond confidently.

##### Forecast patient flow and needs

Oracle Health Command Center Dashboard applies predictive analytics to anticipate patient flow and resource needs, helping leaders coordinate teams, optimize capacity, and maintain efficient, patient-centered care across the enterprise.

### Digital room signage

#### Enhance care coordination with electronic displays outside care suites

Use secure status updates, LED indicators, and timely digital displays to keep teams aligned. Place the right information at the right time to help enhance coordination, strengthen communication, and support effective care.

##### Secure EHR-agnostic signage

Oracle Health Digital Room Signage displays patient information securely outside rooms, replacing outdated communication methods and keeping clinicians and families informed. Fully EHR-agnostic, our solution integrates seamlessly across care environments.

##### At-a-glance patient awareness

Oracle Health Digital Room Signage uses LED light bars and digital displays to signal patient status, room conditions, and staff presence, providing quick, at-a-glance updates without the need to enter rooms.

### Patient flow

#### Manage patient flow with near real-time visibility

Monitor near real-time data and use intelligent bed management capabilities to gain insights into patient movement and capacity. Optimize throughput, accelerate admissions, ease boarding delays, and create smooth care transitions while using resources efficiently.

##### Automate patient flow tasks

Oracle Health Patient Flow automates equipment requests, transport services, and environmental services (EVS), helping limit reliance on phone calls to enhance efficiencies and relieve operational bottlenecks across your enterprise.

##### Optimize staff planning

Oracle Health Patient Flow provides near real-time, contextually aware metrics—including bed use, length of stay, pending transfers, and discharge details—to help support informed staffing decisions and drive clinical, operational, and financial excellence.

### Patient observer

#### Enhance safety with intelligent video monitoring

Monitor patients with smart video surveillance and near real-time notifications to help identify risks, intervene quickly, and prevent incidents. Enable timely actions to help enhance patient safety while limiting costs from falls and extended hospital stays.

##### Limit notification fatigue

Oracle Health Patient Observer customizes motion zones to track patients individually, helping limit unnecessary notifications and alert fatigue while keeping the care team focused on patient safety.

##### 3D monitoring

Oracle Health Patient Observer leverages advanced 3D camera technology to see depth, respond to verbal commands, and assess situations, helping teams intervene effectively and provide safe, responsive care.

##### Timely mobile notifications

Oracle Health Patient Observer integrates with Oracle Health EHR Nursing Mobility to send notifications directly to caregivers at the bedside, enabling them to respond quickly to emerging risks and support patient safety.

[Explore nursing mobility](https://www.oracle.com/health/clinical-suite/#rc30p4)

### Transfer center

#### Streamline patient transfers

Simplify admissions by helping automate clinical assessments, enhance team communication, and centralize documentation. Connect workflows and coordinate information to enable safe, timely patient transfers to the right care setting.

##### Transfer insights

Oracle Health Transfer Center tracks admission requests and care team communications with time-stamped notes, creating a clear record of admission or decline decisions and the reasons behind them for full transparency.

##### Seamless interfacility transfers

Oracle Health Transfer Center uses physician orders and pre-populated documentation to help support safe, coordinated transfers across facilities. Limit manual steps and centralize information to streamline the process and help patients reach the right setting without delays.

# **_What is an Operational Command Center?_**

![A male doctor wearing glasses and a white coat with a stethoscope, smiling and holding a tablet, with medical-themed icons in the background.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/64b045bf07de7b3e0c3106dc/0dca7ef2-0526-413e-9e78-7cf9146c486c/New+Site+Main+Graphics.jpg)

### In the fast-paced world of healthcare, staying ahead requires precision, efficiency, and the ability to adapt quickly to changing circumstances. That's where an Operational Command Center comes into play. An Operational Command Center is a solution designed to be the beating heart of your hospital or healthcare system's success. It can help your facility improve coordination, utilize analytic intelligence, expand visibility, and aggregate information from various sources. These capabilities empower your organization to make informed, proactive, and strategic decisions.

# _Your organization might have a wealth of data from your existing technology, but_ _how__effectively__are you using it?_

# **_Working Smarter, Not Harder With Your Data_**

A fundamental pillar of our Operational Command Center is an advanced data analytics engine, empowering your hospital with unparalleled insights into every aspect of your operations. From patient flow to resource allocation, our Command Centers provide insights in real time, enabling you to make informed decisions instantly and with confidence. The holistic data visibility facilitated by Command Centers aggregates information from across the ecosystem, which streamlines the strategic decision-making process and connects previously siloed areas and information.

A Command Center can help you use existing data to drive action. Real-time and predictive analytics facilitate informed awareness to resolve barriers, assign accountability, and identify priorities. Meanwhile, omnichannel access to retrospective information can also reveal trends and recurring issues, fueling continuous improvement of your operations.

# **_Streamlined_****_Workflow Management_**

Efficiency is the backbone of any successful healthcare system, and our Operational Command Center solutions streamline your operational workflows like never before. By centralizing information and fostering cross-departmental communication, you can eliminate barriers to patient progression and ensure seamless coordination, resulting in improved patient care and increased staff satisfaction.

The Command Center positively impacts nearly every area of your organization. Command Centers are designed to help your hospital or healthcare system:

## **_Improve:_**

#### -       Patient Throughput

#### -       Bed Capacity

#### -       OR Efficiency

#### -       Care Delivery standardization

#### -       Patient experience and satisfaction

#### -       Staff performance and satisfaction

## **_Decrease:_**

#### -       Length of Stay (LOS)

#### -       Wait times

#### -       Cost of care

#### -       Delays in care

#### -       Emergency Department diversion and boarding

#### -       Turnaround times for bed requests and imaging

# **_Adaptive_** **_Resource Management:_**

### In times of high demand, your hospital's success hinges on the ability to manage resources optimally. Our Operational Command Center empowers you with predictive capabilities, enabling you to anticipate surges and allocate staff, equipment, and space accordingly. Say goodbye to overburdened departments and underutilized resources.

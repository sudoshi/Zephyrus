## Technology, Seamlessly Integrated Into Care

## Transforming the Way Hospitals Work

Artisight continuously senses, interprets, and responds through multimodal intelligence, enabling virtual nursing, virtual observation, ambient documentation, tele-consult, surgical case length prediction, and more across the care continuum.

![person with voice recognition icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Voice-Recognition-Icon.png)

#### Computer Vision and Voice Recognition

Automatically observes room activity and captures key clinical interactions to reduce manual documentation and support safer care.

![audio and visual icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Real-Time-Audio-Icon.png)

#### Real-time Two-Way Audio/Video

Enables instant, secure communication between care teams, patients, and remote clinicians without disrupting workflows.

![integrations gear icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Customized-Clinical-Integrations.png)

#### Customized Clinical Integrations

Seamlessly connects with EHRs and other hospital systems to fit existing workflows and eliminate duplicate work.

![computer vision icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/AI-Edge-Computing-Icon.png)

#### AI Edge Compute

Processes data locally inside the hospital for real-time insights, low latency, and enhanced data privacy.

![geo tracking icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/RTLS-Icon.png)

#### RTLS

Tracks equipment, staff, and patient movement to improve coordination, reduce delays, and optimize resource use.

![person icon with graph icon](https://artisight.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Touchless-Vials-Icon.png)

#### Touchless Vitals

Collects vital signs without physical contact, reducing interruptions while supporting continuous monitoring.

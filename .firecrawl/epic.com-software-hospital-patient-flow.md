# Hospital Patient Flow

Track and manage capacity across your organization with Grand Central to make better use of beds and clinical staff.

Capacity Command Center

Capacity across your system is transparent and shown in real-time. Monitor performance goals and staff productivity and quickly find opportunities for open beds across hospitals.

Transport

Automatically assign transport requests within the hospital based on proximity and priority, and automatically create transport requests for appointments and transfers so staff don’t have to. Transporters use Epic’s mobile app, Rover, to document transports and update patients’ location in real time.

![](https://www.epic.com/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fmedia.epic.com%2Fepicdotcom%2Fdirectus%2FsoftwareConcepts%2FBB2BA32D-C859-47BC-A322-18BAE446D2EC%2FTransport%403x-8.png&w=3840&q=75)

Transport

Environmental Services

Make staff aware of required cleaning protocols automatically based on isolation information in the patient’s chart. Automatically assign cleaning requests based on staff skills and request priority to improve efficiency. Environmental Services staff use Epic’s mobile app, Rover, to document cleans in real time.

Ochsner Health opened a centralized patient flow center to increase the efficiency of inter- and intra-hospital transfers. Patients receive the right level of care sooner, an improvement that saves an estimated 56 lives annually.

Mobile Teamwork for Faster Bed Turnaround

By using Epic’s mobile app, Rover, to connect housekeeping staff and clinicians, Parkview Health has reduced turnaround time by 7 minutes per bed, so patients can be roomed sooner.

# Operations IQ Platform

How do you operate? TeleTracking’s Operations IQ Platform is the platform that provides capabilities for optimizing connectivity as well as visibility into owned, affiliated, and unaffiliated healthcare entities. The result is integrated care coordination, automated and accelerated flow, and improved operations within and across care networks.

![](https://eadn-wc03-11006557.nxedge.io/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/hoc-1-scaled.jpg)

## Numbers you can trust

Every day, over 200 healthcare systems – including the top three largest health systems in the US – and more than 1,000 individual hospitals, rely on our Operations IQ Platform platform to optimize their operations, better support staff, and enhance patient care.

## End-to-end solutions

Our platform works across your entire healthcare ecosystem, providing real-time visibility into everything from patient admissions and care delivery to resource utilization and care transitions – all while complementing your existing clinical documentation process.

[**Access** \\
Access – part of TeleTracking’s Operations IQ Platform – gives health systems enterprise-wide visibility and AI-powered workflows to ensure every patient reaches the right level of care, at the right facility, without unnecessary delay.\\
Learn more](https://www.teletracking.com/healthcare-operations-iq-platform/access/) [**Throughput** \\
ED boarding, delayed discharges, idle beds, overwhelmed staff — these aren’t isolated problems. They’re symptoms of disconnected patient flow. The Operations IQ Platform’s Throughput module coordinates every step of the acute care journey, from admission to discharge, so your hospital operates as a unified system rather than a collection of competing bottlenecks.\\
Learn more](https://www.teletracking.com/healthcare-operations-iq-platform/throughput/) [**Ambulatory** \\
Your fastest growing site of care – and your least coordinated. Ambulatory care now represents 50% or more of health system revenue, yet most systems still manage referrals, scheduling, and capacity in silos. The Operations IQ Platform’s Ambulatory solution gives you the coordinated visibility and AI-powered orchestration to turn that growth into margin.\\
Learn more](https://www.teletracking.com/healthcare-operations-iq-platform/ambulatory/) [**Data and Analytics** \\
One of our superpowers is informing your care access, delivery, transition, and discharge operations with data-driven situational awareness. Our analytic reporting and dashboards enable you to see real-time capacities and patient movement down to the unit – and compare them to predicted census and throughput numbers.\\
Learn more](https://www.teletracking.com/healthcare-operations-cloud/data-analytics/)

## End-to-end patient flow

We work across the entire patient care journey, creating a holistic approach to integrated healthcare operations powered by the latest operational technology and informed by decades of clinical experience.

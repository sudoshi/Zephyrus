## Edge-to-Cloud Ambient Sensors Built to Scale

care.ai ambient sensor arrays securely support patient well-being and provide real-time AI
guidance while connecting all hospital departments to enable proactive support and
communication.


[Learn more about Always-Aware Ambient Sensors![Right Arrow](https://www.care.ai/images/big-right-arrow.svg)](https://www.care.ai/sensors.html)

#### Easy IoT Installation ![Left Arrow](https://www.care.ai/images/left-arrow.svg)

Ultra-secure and built with healthcare-grade materials, care.ai sensors are plug-and-play,
work with existing network connections and TVs and are easy to deploy and provision.


#### Low Latency + Low Network Load![Left Arrow](https://www.care.ai/images/left-arrow.svg)

care.ai’s Always-Aware Sensors combine local AI inferencing, virtual care support, and
ambient monitoring on a powerful edge compute neural network, keeping local response times
fast with low latency and low network load.


#### AI Federated Learning![Left Arrow](https://www.care.ai/images/left-arrow.svg)

care.ai sensors are always learning and always improving, securely sharing that learning
from one sensor to the next. Many networked AI brains act together as one to serve and
protect clinicians and patients.
